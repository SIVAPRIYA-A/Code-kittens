SAVE AND SUSTAINABLY USE OF OCEAN AND MARINE RESOURCES

Our planet Earth covers 70% of water and 30% of land. Yet we have managed to do damage to the resources. We must protect them by eliminating the pollution and overfishing in the oceans.

Marine Life Pollution

By 2025,prevent and significantly reduce marine pollution in all kinds of works especially land based activities affects a lot,including debris and nutrient pollution. Explore the beauty of under water dolphins and fishes and other creatures affects a lot.

Acidified Pollution

Increase in scientific knowledge develop research capacity and transfer marine technology leads to a wide range acidified pollution in underwater. Minimize the impacts of ocean acidification including through enhanced scientific co-operations.

Plastic and Sewage Pollution

Usage of plastic bags and other chemical based products are mixing with sea water makes many changes in marine biodiversity. By this,many creatures in the sea water are affected a lot and human consumes those water are affected by manyy diseases.

Sustainable use of marine resources

By 2030,increase the economic benefits to small island developing states and least developed countries from the sustainable use of marine resources, including through sustainanble management of fisheries and aqua-culture and tourism.

OverFishing

By 2020, prohibit certain forms of fisheries subsidies which contributes to over capacity and overfishing and eliminate subsidies that contribute to illegal, and unreported and un-regulated fishing and refrain from introducing new such subsides, recoginize that appropriate and effective special and differential treatment for developing and least developed countries.
